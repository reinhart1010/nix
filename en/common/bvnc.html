bvnc

A GUI tool for browsing for SSH/VNC servers on the local network. See also: bssh and bshell. More information: https://manned.org/bvnc.

  • Browse for VNC servers:

bvnc

  • Browse for SSH servers:

bvnc --ssh

  • Browse for both VNC and SSH servers:

bvnc --shell

  • Browse for VNC servers in a specified domain:

bvnc --domain domain
