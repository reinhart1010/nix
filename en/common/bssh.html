bssh

A GUI tool for browsing for SSH/VNC servers on the local network. See also: bvnc and bshell. More information: https://manned.org/bssh.

  • Browse for SSH servers:

bssh

  • Browse for VNC servers:

bssh --vnc

  • Browse for both SSH and VNC servers:

bssh --shell

  • Browse for SSH servers in a specified domain:

bssh --domain domain
