base32

Encode or decode file or standard input to/from Base32, to standard output. More information: https://www.gnu.org/software/coreutils/base32.

  • Encode a file:

base32 filename

  • Decode a file:

base32 --decode filename

  • Encode from stdin:

somecommand | base32

  • Decode from stdin:

somecommand | base32 --decode
