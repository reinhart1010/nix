rtv

Reddit Terminal Viewer. Use arrow keys to navigate. Right and Left to view and return from a submission, respectively. More information: https://github.com/michael-lazar/rtv.

  • Open the front page:

/front

  • Open a subreddit:

/r/subreddit_name

  • Expand/collapse comments:

<Space>

  • Open link:

o

  • Log in:

u

  • Display help:

?
