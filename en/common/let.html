let

Evaluate arithmetic expressions in shell. Supports variables, operators, and conditional expressions. More information: https://manned.org/let.

  • Evaluate a simple arithmetic expression:

let "result = a + b"

  • Use post-increment and assignment in an expression:

let "x++"

  • Use conditional operator in an expression:

let "result = (x > 10) ? x : 0"

  • Display help:

let --help
