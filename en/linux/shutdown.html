shutdown

Shutdown and reboot the system. More information: https://manned.org/shutdown.8.

  • Power off ([h]alt) immediately:

shutdown -h now

  • [r]eboot immediately:

shutdown -r now

  • [r]eboot in 5 minutes:

shutdown -r +5 &

  • Shutdown at 1:00 pm (Uses 24[h] clock):

shutdown -h 13:00

  • [c]ancel a pending shutdown/reboot operation:

shutdown -c
