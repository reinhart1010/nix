steamos-chroot

Switch root directory in a SteamOS environment. More information: https://gitlab.com/users/evlaV/projects.

  • Switch to the other A/B partition:

steamos-chroot --partset other

  • Switch to a partition on another drive:

steamos-chroot --disk /dev/sdX --partset A|B

  • Display help:

steamos-chroot --help
