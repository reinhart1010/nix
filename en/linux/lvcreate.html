lvcreate

Create a logical volume in an existing volume group. A volume group is a collection of logical and physical volumes. See also: lvm. More information: https://manned.org/lvcreate.

  • Create a logical volume of 10 gigabytes in the volume group vg1:

lvcreate -L 10G vg1

  • Create a 1500 megabyte linear logical volume named mylv in the volume group vg1:

lvcreate -L 1500 -n mylv vg1

  • Create a logical volume called mylv that uses 60% of the total space in volume group vg1:

lvcreate -l 60%VG -n mylv vg1

  • Create a logical volume called mylv that uses all the unallocated space in the volume group vg1:

lvcreate -l 100%FREE -n mylv vg1
