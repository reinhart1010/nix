terminator

Arrange multiple GNOME terminals in one window. More information: https://gnome-terminator.org/.

  • Start terminator window:

terminator

  • Start with a fullscreen window:

terminator -f

  • Split terminals horizontally:

<Ctrl> + <Shift> + O

  • Split terminals vertically:

<Ctrl> + <Shift> + E

  • Open new tab:

<Ctrl> + <Shift> + T
