wl-paste

Paste content in Wayland clipboard. See also: wl-copy, xclip. More information: https://github.com/bugaevc/wl-clipboard.

  • Paste the contents of the clipboard:

wl-paste

  • Paste the contents of the primary clipboard (highlighted text):

wl-paste --primary

  • Write the contents of the clipboard to a file:

wl-paste > path/to/file

  • Pipe the contents of the clipboard to a command:

wl-paste | command
