cointop

Track and monitor cryptocurrencies in the terminal. More information: https://github.com/cointop-sh/cointop.

  • Open TUI:

cointop

  • Clear the cache:

cointop clean

  • Display current holdings legibly:

cointop holdings --human

  • Check price of coin(s):

cointop price --coins coin_name1,coin_name2,â€¦

  • Display version:

cointop version
