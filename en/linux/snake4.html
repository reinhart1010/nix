snake4

Snake game in the terminal. More information: https://manpages.debian.org/snake4/snake4.6.en.html.

  • Start a snake game:

snake4

  • Choose level:

1|2|3|4|5

  • Navigate the snake:

Up|Down|Left|Right arrow key

  • Pause game:

Spacebar

  • Quit game:

q

  • Show the high scores:

snake4 --highscores
