More information: <https://www.wireguard.com/quickstart/>.

`wg-quick up `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">interface_name</span>

- Delete a VPN tunnel:
