nmcli radio

Show the status of radio switches or enable/disable them using NetworkManager. This subcommand can also be called with nmcli r. More information: https://networkmanager.pages.freedesktop.org/NetworkManager/NetworkManager/nmcli.html.

  • Show status of Wi-Fi:

nmcli radio wifi

  • Turn Wi-Fi on or off:

nmcli radio wifi on|off

  • Show status of WWAN:

nmcli radio wwan

  • Turn WWAN on or off:

nmcli radio wwan on|off

  • Show status of both switches:

nmcli radio all

  • Turn both switches on or off:

nmcli radio all on|off
