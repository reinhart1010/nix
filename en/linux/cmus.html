cmus

Command-line Music Player. Use arrow keys to navigate, <enter/return> to select, and numbers 1-8 switch between different views. See also: ncmpcpp, clementine, qmmp. More information: https://cmus.github.io.

  • Open cmus into the specified directory (this will become your new working directory):

cmus path/to/directory

  • Add file/directory to library:

:add path/to/file_or_directory

  • Pause/unpause current song:

c

  • Toggle shuffle mode on/off:

s

  • Quit cmus:

q
