rpicam-still

Capture and store a photo using a Raspberry Pi camera with legacy features missing from rpicam-jpeg. More information: https://www.raspberrypi.com/documentation/computers/camera_software.html#rpicam-still.

  • Capture a photo with different encoding:

rpicam-still -e bmp|png|rgb|yuv420 -o path/to/file.<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">bmp|png|rgb|yuv420}</span>}`

  • Capture a raw image:

rpicam-still -r -o path/to/file.jpg

  • Capture a 100 second exposure image:

rpicam-still -o path/to/file.jpg --shutter 100000
