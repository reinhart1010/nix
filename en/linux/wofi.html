wofi

An application launcher for wlroots-based Wayland compositors, similar to rofi and dmenu. More information: https://hg.sr.ht/~scoopta/wofi.

  • Show the list of apps:

wofi --show drun

  • Show the list of all commands:

wofi --show run

  • Pipe a list of items to stdin and print the selected item to stdout:

printf "Choice1\nChoice2\nChoice3" | wofi --dmenu
