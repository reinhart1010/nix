base64

Encode and decode using Base64 representation. More information: https://keith.github.io/xcode-man-pages/base64.1.html.

  • Encode a file:

base64 --input=plain_file

  • Decode a file:

base64 --decode --input=base64_file

  • Encode from stdin:

echo -n "plain_text" | base64

  • Decode from stdin:

echo -n base64_text | base64 --decode
