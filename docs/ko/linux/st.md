더 많은 정보: <https://st.suckless.org>.

`st`

`st -o `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">경로/대상/파일</span>` -e `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">명령어 인수1 인수2</span>
