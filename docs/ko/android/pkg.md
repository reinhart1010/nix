<hr># pkg

Termux용 패키지 관리 유틸리티.
더 많은 정보: <https://wiki.termux.com/wiki/Package_Management>.

- 설치된 모든 패키지 업그레이드:

`pkg upgrade`

- 패키지 설치:

`pkg install `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">패키지</span>

- 패키지 삭제:

`pkg uninstall `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">패키지</span>

- 패키지 재설치:

`pkg reinstall `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">패키지</span>

- 패키지 검색:

`pkg search `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">패키지</span>
