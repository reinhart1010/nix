더 많은 정보: <https://www.gnu.org/software/bash/manual/bash.html#index-enable>.

- 내장 목록을 출력:

`enable`

`enable -n `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">명령어</span>
