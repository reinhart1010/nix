<hr># pamtopnm

Convert a PAM image to an equivalent PNM image.
More information: <https://netpbm.sourceforge.net/doc/pamtopnm.html>.

- Convert a PAM image to an equivalent PNM image, i.e. a PBM, PGM or PPM image:

`pamtopnm `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">path/to/image.pam</span>` > `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">path/to/output.pbm|pgm|ppm</span>

- Display version:

`pamtopnm -version`
