<hr># gdaldem

Tool to analyze and visualize digital elevation models (DEM).
More information: <https://gdal.org/programs/gdaldem>.

- Compute the hillshade of a DEM:

`gdaldem hillshade `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">path/to/input.tif</span>` `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">path/to/output.tif</span>

- Compute the slope of a DEM:

`gdaldem slope `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">path/to/input.tif</span>` `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">path/to/output.tif</span>

- Compute the aspect of a DEM:

`gdaldem aspect `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">path/to/input.tif</span>` `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">path/to/output.tif</span>
