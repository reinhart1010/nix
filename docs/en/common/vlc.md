# vlc

Cross-platform multimedia player.
See also: `mpv`, `mplayer`, `ytfzf`.
More information: <https://wiki.videolan.org/Documentation:Command_line/>.

- Play a file:

`vlc `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">path/to/file</span>

- Play in fullscreen:

`vlc --fullscreen `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">path/to/file</span>

- Play muted:

`vlc --no-audio `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">path/to/file</span>

- Play repeatedly:

`vlc --loop `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">path/to/file</span>

- Play video from a URL:

`vlc `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oHg5SJYRHA0</span>
