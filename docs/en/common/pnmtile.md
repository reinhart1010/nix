<hr># pnmtile

Replicate an image to fill a specified area.
More information: <https://netpbm.sourceforge.net/doc/pnmtile.html>.

- Replicate an image to fill an area of the specified dimensions:

`pnmtile `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">width</span>` `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">height</span>` `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">path/to/input.pnm</span>` > `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">path/to/output.pnm</span>
