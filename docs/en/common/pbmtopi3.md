<hr># pbmtopi3

Convert a PBM image to an Atari Degas PI3 image.
See also: `pi3topbm`.
More information: <https://netpbm.sourceforge.net/doc/pbmtopi3.html>.

- Convert a PBM image to an Atari Degas PI3 image:

`pbmtopi3 `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">path/to/image.pbm</span>` > `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">path/to/atari_image.pi3</span>
