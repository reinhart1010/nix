# silentcast

Silent screencast creator. Saves in `.mkv` and animated GIF formats.
More information: <https://github.com/colinkeenan/silentcast>.

- Launch silentcast:

`silentcast`

- Launch silentcast on a specific display:

`silentcast --display=`<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">display</span>
