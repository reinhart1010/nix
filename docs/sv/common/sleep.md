Mer information: <https://www.gnu.org/software/coreutils/sleep>.

`sleep `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">sekunder</span>

`sleep `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">minuter</span>`m`

- Fördröj i timmar:

`sleep `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">timmar</span>`h`
