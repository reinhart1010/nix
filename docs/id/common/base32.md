<hr># base32

Lakukan pengodean dan pendekodean terhadap suatu berkas atau `stdin` dari/menuju format Base32, menuju `stdout`.
Informasi lebih lanjut: <https://manned.org/base32>.

- Kodekan isi suatu berkas menuju format Base32:

`base32 `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">jalan/menuju/berkas</span>

- Bungkus luaran Base32 dalam panjang karakter yang tetap (nilai `0` akan menonaktifkan pembungkusan):

`base32 `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">-w|--wrap</span>` `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">0|76|...</span>` `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">jalan/menuju/berkas</span>

- Dekodekan kode Base32 yang tersimpan dalam suatu berkas:

`base32 `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">-d|--decode</span>` `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">jalan/menuju/berkas</span>

- Kodekan isi dari `stdin`:

<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">perintah</span>` | base32`

- Dekodekan kode Base32 yang berasal dari `stdout`:

<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">perintah</span>` | base32 `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">-d|--decode</span>
