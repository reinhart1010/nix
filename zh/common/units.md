<hr># units

提供两个度量单位之间的转换。
更多信息：<https://www.gnu.org/software/units/>.

- 以交互模式运行：

`units`

- 显示两个简单单位之间的转换：

`units `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">quarts(夸脱)</span>` `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">tablespoons(大汤匙)</span>

- 单位与数量之间的转换：

`units "`<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">15 pounds(磅)</span>`" `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">kilograms(公斤)</span>

- 显示两个复合单位之间的转换：

`units "`<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">meters(米) / second(秒)</span>`" "`<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">inches(英尺) / hour(小时)</span>`"`

- 显示具有不同维度的单位之间的转换：

`units "`<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">acres(英亩)</span>`" "`<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">ft(英尺)^2(平方)</span>`"`

- 显示字节乘数的转换：

`units "`<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">15 megabytes(兆字节)</span>`" `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">bytes(字节)</span>
