# basename

移除一个路径的目录部分字符。
更多信息：<https://www.gnu.org/software/coreutils/basename>.

- 仅显示文件名：

`basename `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">路径/到/文件</span>

- 显示路径字符最右边表示目录的字符：

`basename `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">路径/到/目录/</span>

- 展示无后缀的文件名称：

`basename `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">路径/到/文件</span>` `<span class="tldr-var badge badge-pill bg-dark-lm bg-white-dm text-white-lm text-dark-dm font-weight-bold">后缀</span>
